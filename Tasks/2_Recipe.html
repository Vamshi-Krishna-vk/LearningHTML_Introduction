How to make a Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Cheese slices or grated cheese (optional)

Step-by-Step Procedure

  1. Prepare the Bread:

    • Take the bread slices and spread butter on one side of each.
    • Toast lightly if you prefer a crispy texture.

  2. Add the chutney or spread:

    • Spread green chutney, mayonnaise, or ketchup evenly on one side of each bread slice.

  3. Arrange the vegetables:

    • Place cucumber, tomato, onion, and carrot slices over one slice of bread.
    • Sprinkle a pinch of salt and pepper for taste.

  4. Add cheese (optional):

    • Place a cheese slice or sprinkle grated cheese over the vegetables.

  5. Close the sandwich:

    • Cover it with another slice of bread (spread side facing down).

  6. Toast or grill (optional):

    • Apply a little butter on the outer sides and toast or grill until golden brown.

  7. Serve:

    • Cut diagonally or into halves and serve with ketchup, chutney, or any dip of your choice.

Precautions & Tips

  • Use fresh vegetables - wash them thoroughly before slicing.
  • Donâ€™t overload with too many wet ingredients (like tomatoes or sauces) to avoid sogginess.
  • If packing for later, avoid chutney or ketchup inside - carry them separately to keep the sandwich crisp.
  • Use clean utensils and surfaces to maintain hygiene.
  • If giving to kids, cut off hard crusts and ensure vegetables are finely sliced.
